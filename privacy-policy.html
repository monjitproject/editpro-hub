Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

Introduction

EditPro Hub ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website editprohub.com ("the Site").

Important: All editing tools on EditPro Hub process files entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your images, text, and other files are never uploaded to our servers or stored by us.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, we automatically collect certain information when you visit, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring URL
  • IP address (anonymized by Google Analytics)

Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience and serve relevant advertisements. Specifically:

  • Google AdSense uses cookies to serve personalized advertisements based on your browsing behavior.
  • Google Analytics uses cookies to help us understand how visitors interact with our site.
  • Local Storage We use localStorage for theme preferences (dark/light mode). This data stays on your device.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense — Serves advertisements. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on visits to our site and other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • Google Analytics — Tracks aggregated usage data to help us improve the site.

How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and maintain our website
  • To improve user experience based on aggregated analytics
  • To display relevant advertisements that support the free operation of our tools
  • To respond to inquiries sent through our contact page

Data Retention

We do not store any files you upload or process through our tools. All processing occurs client-side in your browser. Analytics data is retained for 26 months as per Google Analytics standard retention policy.

Your Rights

  • Opt-Out of Personalized Ads: Visit Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page or Digital Advertising Alliance.
  • Disable Cookies: You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.
  • Access or Delete Data: Contact us at support@editprohub.com for any data-related requests.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@editprohub.com or through our Contact page.
